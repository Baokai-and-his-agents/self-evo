## Key Findings

### Technical Achievement
The MVP successfully demonstrates:
1. **Event Stream Independence**: A/B/E/G consume identical trade events, only sizing differs
2. **No Lookahead**: All indicators use only past bars
3. **Deterministic**: Fixed seed produces reproducible results
4. **Conditional Analysis**: Framework correctly computes P(win|stop_count) and confidence intervals
5. **Permutation Test**: G placebo correctly shuffles sizing-state mapping

- Generated: 1 trade event over 750 days
- Result: Pipeline works but statistics meaningless (n=1)
- Cause: Synthetic data may lack sufficient breakout patterns
